About

I'm a multidisciplinary graphic designer with 5+ years of experience across brand identity, packaging, editorial and digital design — with direct experience designing for a FinTech platform. I've led design end-to-end for clients across the UK and Nigeria: building visual identity systems, shaping packaging and label suites, producing campaign and social creative, and keeping every asset accountable to the brand it belongs to.

My background in Data Analytics for Business Intelligence gives me a genuine, practical read on how to turn complex or data-heavy content into clear visual stories — useful for dashboards and reports, but the same instinct for clarity runs through every packaging label and campaign layout I design.
